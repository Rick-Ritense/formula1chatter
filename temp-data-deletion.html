Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Facebook Platform policies, we provide this tool for you to request deletion of your data from our Formula 1 Chatter Championship application.

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to: datadeletion@f1chatter.com

Required Information:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Facebook User ID (if you know it)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Happens Next

  1. We will verify your identity as the account owner
  2. Delete all your personal data from our systems within 30 days
  3. Send a confirmation email when the process is complete

Data We Will Delete

  • Your user profile information
  • All race predictions you've made
  • Your leaderboard scores and rankings
  • Any stored preferences or settings
  • All associated metadata and logs

This data deletion mechanism is provided in compliance with Facebook's Platform Terms.

You can also revoke permissions to our app through your Facebook settings at any time.
